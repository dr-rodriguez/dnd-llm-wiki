
# Session 2026-06-18

We head out to the AA guns to help clear them of hostiles.
We try to be stealthy, but aren’t very successful. We are delayed and arrive an hour after we intended to.

We arrive at what appears to have been a church.
There is a fountain with a chimera statue and some hippocampus statues.

Bramble unlocks a door but on opening gets shot at by some enemy robot troops.
She’s able to close the door quickly, but does get hit.

It’s combat time!

Maxim puts a Minor Illusion of Brambleberry at the door to confuse them while Zinjaro preps a Sacred Flame in case he spots an enemy. A robot peeks through the door but avoids the Sacred Flame cast, he doesn’t realize the illusionary Bramble is fake. They throw a flashbang- Maxim and Villhelm are blinded and stunned. Another one shoots at the illusion and realizes it’s fake.

Ogra rages while another robot comes from another door.
Zinjaro gets shot at, but uses Light Snare to restrain them and cast another Sacred Flame at them while moving away. Aolis casts a Witch Bolt and summons their Hound of Ill Omen. Bramble throws their daggers into the room.

The robots remotely call for backup, while Villhelm recovers from the flashbang and goes to punch the one that came from the side.
Several robots have been coming from the corner so Zinjaro throws a Fireball at the corner in the hopes of catching some. Bramble takes out one of the robots with her daggers.

A large robot breaks through a wall and fires at Villhelm.
