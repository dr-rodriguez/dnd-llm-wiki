# Quests

This page tracks the major objectives and current missions of the party.

## Current Objective: The Siege of Tessalia (April 2026)
Following their successful mission to the Drakenweld volcano to clear "the rot" and defeat a radioactive dragon, the party has returned to **Tessalia** to find it under siege by **automatons** from a massive **Tower Island** carrier-ship. 

### Current Tasks:
- [ ] **Guild Briefing:** Report to the **Adventurer’s Guild** and Maggie to understand the nature of the invasion.
- [ ] **Protect the Royalty:** Locate and protect **Queen Tessalia**, who is currently hiding underground.
- [ ] **Counter-Attack:** Address the automaton threat and investigate the connection to the **Tower Island** and its rogue technology.

## Past Major Objectives

### The Drakenweld Excursion (October 2025 – April 2026)
- **Objective:** Travel to the Drakenweld to investigate ancient technology and the **Net** (a planar wall).
- **Key Outcome:** Defeated a radioactive dragon in a volcanic caldera to clear the "rot" affecting local nature spirits.
- **Key Contacts:** Met **Danyel** (a clone of an ancient scientist) and **Tick/Screeck** (insectoid technical specialists).

### The Guild Mission to Tessalia (January 2025)
- **Objective:** Initial mission to check the status of the **Tessalia Guild** and protect the young monarch, **Queen Tessalia**.
- **Key Outcome:** Successfully established themselves as reliable adventurers in the city.

## Recurring & Secondary Goals
- **Sofia AI:** Investigate the ancient **Sofia AI** and its relationship with the royal line of Tessalia.
- **Zinjaro's Worship:** Zinjaro seeks to worship **Sol'Ar** through life's pleasures and the fulfillment of his divine "sun-touched" role.
- **Maxim's Origins:** Explore Maxim’s history as a changeling and his connection to the ancient **Anwey** infiltrators.
