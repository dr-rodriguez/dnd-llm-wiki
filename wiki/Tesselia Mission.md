# Tesselia Mission

The mission to return to **[[wiki/Locations#Tesselia|Tesselia]]** was assigned by the Helines Adventurer's Guild leader, **[[wiki/Characters#Lady Poomf|Lady Poomf]]**. 

## Background and Motivation
In early 2025, the party met with two "Island Dwellers" named **Nock** and **Screeck**, who had acquired "Battle Plans" and "Interesting Notes." These documents revealed dangerous enemy activity, specifically highlighting "sky creatures, metal bodies," "sky ships," and humanoid droids involved in warfare on the frontlines. 

Due to concerns that this enemy activity could sever communication with the West continent, Lady Poomf assigned the party a mission to head to Tesselia. Tesselia was of particular interest as they had previously provided the party with their Fabricator Cube.

## The Objectives
Lady Poomf's explicit instructions were to:
1. **Check the Guild's Status:** Go to the Tesselia Adventurer's Guild, check their status, and report back via magic or physical presence.
2. **Protect the Royalty:** If they encountered resistance from the enemy, they were ordered to strike them down and ensure the protection of both the guild and the young monarch, **[[wiki/Characters#Queen Tesselia|Queen Tesselia]]**.

## Arrival and the Siege
After extensive travel (and crashing on an island with a volcano and a radioactive dragon), the party finally arrived at the rocky islands outside Tesselia in April 2026 via an airship piloted by **Tick**. 

Upon their arrival, they discovered that Tesselia was under siege by the very threat the Island Dwellers had warned about: automatons from a massive **Tower Island** carrier-ship. Magic messages were blocked, proving Lady Poomf's concerns about communication to be well-founded, and forcing the party to physically intervene to help the sheltering guild and locate the hiding royalty. It was later revealed that this attack was orchestrated by **[[wiki/Characters#Odin AI|Odin AI]]** in an attempt to harvest **[[wiki/Characters#Sofia AI|Sofia AI]]**'s AI core. The siege culminated in a massive "Saturation Bombardment" by Odin's ships.
