# Session: 2025-06-19

They say the ghoul upstairs is some kind of pseudo-lich, much more powerful.

Taylor, the cleric, comes to talk to [[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]]. She is a satyr.  
Lady Nora \- goddess of trickery and the night, maybe isn't a god? A cult of personality? Her followers like to keep it vague.  
They bullshit about religion while getting drunk on wine from the alchemy jug. We finish the gallon of wine but end up fine, only a little buzzed.

Zinjaro casts Death Ward on [[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] just in case, though they are technically tankier than Maxim.  
Maxim and Aolis are sharing damage due to the bonding rings.

Maxim and Margaret take the first watch.  
Lavelor casts a Leomond's Tiny Hut for their team.

Aolis hasn't told the team yet about the drow attack.

Maxim and Maggie talk a bit about their journey. He mentions Wobbles and she guesses he's a kobold.   
She's been to [[wiki/Locations#Drakenweld|Drakenweld]]. She tells us it's very cold, rocky soil but very snowy.

[[wiki/Locations#Light|Light]]- the great city, built back during the dragon wars. East after the fork north of the pass. The west fork likely goes to the demon portal.   
She admits they're looking for someone over something.  
Maxim and Maggie play some dice games.

They start hearing a banging from the door as the barricade starts to break.  
A fight ensues against 4 ghasts.

Maggie is wielding ancient tech- a phase knife  
Wendy rages and essentially turns into a wendigo.  
Taylor is a twilight domain cleric and uses Turn Undead.

We win the fight, but Maxim wanders north and triggers another group of undead to hit us.

As a note, Taylor has used channel divinity Twilight Sanctuary which pushes out temp HP at the end of player turns. I'll have to remind the DM about this. We can take the average of 1d6 \+ 11 which is 14\.
