# Session 2026-06-04

**Date**: 2026-06-04
**Source**: [[raw/2026/2026-06-04.md|Source]]

## Summary
The party leaves the tower and heads back to the [[wiki/Locations#Adventurers Guild Hall|Guild]]. Along the way, they run into some familiar faces from the original party: Greed (Zarali), [[wiki/Characters#Juneberry|Juneberry]], [[wiki/Characters#Tsuki|Tsuki]], and [[wiki/Characters#Angela Nevermore|Lady Nevermore]]. Maggie is with them. The party explains their mission and their need to head to the castle. They report their success regarding the scion in yellow and the book, receiving a reward of 300 platinum and 1000 gold. 

During a short rest, they discover [[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]]'s shadow quirk: his heart only beats once per minute. Aolis shares some of his backstory about leaving the Underdark, and mentions his mother has been sending agents to track him down. [[wiki/Characters#Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] suggests overthrowing them, but Aolis believes it wouldn't go well due to history. Maxim is part of aristocracy, but it is money-based, unlike Aolis's lineage-based aristocracy. [[wiki/Characters#Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]]'s town is communal and works to protect the World Tree.

They travel with Nevermore towards the city center where security is heightened. Through a secret tunnel, they enter an underground facility. There, they see Team B (Melody, Taylor, Lavelor, Wendy) and a bloodied fox and bird ([[wiki/Characters#Shar|Shar]] and [[wiki/Characters#Soren|Soren]]). 

Nevermore leads them to a room with [[wiki/Characters#Queen Tesselia|Queen Tesselia]] (now 13 years old), Margaret, and [[wiki/Characters#Amber Lyre|Amber Lyre]]. They explain their presence (checking on the ruler, offering aid) and mention their run-ins with AI. The Queen summons [[wiki/Characters#Sofia AI|Sofia AI]], who appears as a PS2-graphics hologram from a skittering robot. Sofia controls another facility impacted by volcanic activity. She apologizes for the other AIs' actions and explains she is different and shackled. Sofia is Odin's counterpart; she headed the facilities project to protect humanity to escape to space, while Odin headed Project Horizon. Odin is currently cyberattacking her multiple times an hour. 

Sofia projects hardlight and can touch Maxim. She explains that the robots from the Tower Islands are coming to harvest her AI Core (her brain), which would make her a slave to Odin. Other AIs have either joined Odin or been destroyed. She has gene-sequenced the party, knowing who Maxim is, and mentions Aolis was already in the system from a previous scan.

Suddenly, the systems crash, green screens turn red, and a warning sounds: "Saturation Bombardment is beginning. That’s a lot of ships."
