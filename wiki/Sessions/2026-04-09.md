# Session: 2026-04-09

We exit the volcano. The kobolds seem organized into smaller factions instead of the larger group. We avoid them as we make our way back.

The ship is mostly repaired after 3 days or so.
[[wiki/Characters#Brambleberry|Brambleberry]] tries to contact the dryad, but doesn't know how to reach out. [[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] recommends wine.
Ogra is very wounded and wants to take a nap.

Maxim explains what happened to Tick and Screek.
Tick produces some radioactive vial that is produced as a byproduct of their technology.
Tick produces some medical ointment and helps Ogra and Bramble.

Zinjaro goes to find bananas, but gets lost instead. 4 hours pass.
He got pricked by some thorn and fell asleep.
The others argue that he shouldn't be climbing trees. He demonstrates how "good" he is... but falls and hurts himself anyways.

Bramble attempts to contact the nature spirit with Zinjaro's help. Zinjaro recommends wine.
The dryad appears and takes the bottle (1 of the 3 wine bottles from the vampire's lair).
She says we've done well, but does want us to leave as soon as we can.
She seems to upgrade Bramble's psychic blades (they now do 2d6 now).

The next day we leave on the repaired airship. It's a bit faster now thanks to the magic on the island.

Tick and Bramble have a conversation about connections to nature. In the past, they had nature warriors. They instead went for the usefulness of technology over nature.
Techarch- high-ranking political individuals
They believe others are afraid of technology but they are not, so they should be in charge.
There used to be traders to sell trinkets and interact more with the world, but they also were spies for the Techarchs.
There are stories of moving suits of armor (this was after Maxim's time).

Looks like it will be mainly nutrient bars for the rest of the trip (and Zinjaro's magic bananas).
They chat about taste, they don't have tongues.
"Rootbear"- can eat literal minerals (rocks)

Tick asks about Bramble's earring.
She used it to distinguish themselves from other centaurs and horsegirls.
Tick has no adornments- her outfit is regulation and she cannot personalize her kit. Apparently the Techarchs have colorful robes, but she thinks they are foolish.
Ogra shows off his tattoos. But Tick's training prevents her from seeing it useful.

We spot a boxy shape in the sky.
It's another ship, much stronger.
A carrier-ship from the Tower. These are not allies.
Tick wonders if it's a patrol vessel, but it's a very large ship.
We are faster but are catching up to them- they are going the same direction as we are. They are not responding to our hails.

We get closer, though Tick keeps us at a distance. Their cannons are about the size of half our ship. They have massive bay doors that can swing open- those would be for battalions of ground troops.
The ship doesn't react to our passing.

In about a day, we arrive at some small rocky islands outside Tesselia.
We see smoke in the distance- from fires.
Tesselia appears to be under siege.

We're still in the air. There are a few other airships in the area.
Tick lowers the ship near the north-eastern part of the city, in a clearing. We're outside the wall, but there are gaps in the wall so it shouldn't be hard to get inside.
We're supposed to go to the Adventurer's Guild and find out what is going on.

The streets are empty and there is smoke and rain.

There are lots of people sheltering in the Adventurer's Guild.
We talk to the tiefling (Maggie)
Automatons from the sky invaded some days ago. We know they are some rogue elements from the Tower Island folks.
Royalty is hiding underground.
The Guild is focused on local tasks, rescuing people, digging through rubble.
Magic messages haven't been able to get out so they're having to send people physically with letters.
She'll be reaching out to see if they can set up a meeting with the royalty.

A group of adventurers come in ([[wiki/Characters#Soten|Soten]], [[wiki/Characters#Shar|Shar]], [[wiki/Characters#Tsuki|Tsuki]]) and hand some information to Maggie.
