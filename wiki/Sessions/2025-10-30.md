# Session: 2025-10-30

We continue exploring the dungeon.  
We find a locked chest and Bramble unlocks it.  
There's a dusty trap but [[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] and Bramble avoid it.  
There is a magic seal- Arcane Lock.  
There are 3 books.  
There is a compartment in the back that has a small pouch.

Fancy cloak with clasp for a gem  
A bag of flesh of some kind  
All books are without title, hand-bound  
Super huge tome- blank pages then eventually some scant writing forming some sort of glyph. Maxim remembers a class about codexes- teaching non-spellcasters magic, but doesn't remember much from it. Aolis confirms it is, but can't tell what spell it is for.  
Medium book- has writing, novelization of old legend: snowy town, evil presence comes, someone says maybe not evil, falls in love with evil, both burned at the stake  
Final book- wizard spellbook, they can't read it

Maxim pulls out the tuner and sees 3 signals: Maxim, Portafab (Ogra), something else...  
Under the bed there is a wooden box- that's one of the tuner signals  
It's a standard issue lock from ancient times

While Maxim is unlocking, [[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] investigates the flesh- it's several different people. There are no on-the-books necromancy that require this.  
Aolis puts on the fancy cloak- a cloak of billowing\!  
[[wiki/Characters#Thadius|Thadius]] finishes some infusions, including making a homunculus servant (Zipsy)

In the box: unpowered data tablet, bottle of pills.   
Bottle label: sulfucides  
Opening the bottle smells like brimstone  
Ogra takes a pill to find out what they do. He spits it back out- it's like drinking fire. Maybe activated by liquid? As the pill hits the ground it starts dissolving the floor.

We make our way downstairs through some double doors.  
We found a boat and some brackish water.  
We found some people in a large room. They look... viscous, undead....  
Lord Marcus Nevara, sitting on a throne.  
He asks why we are invading his home.  
He stopped caring about the village a millenia ago. Demons haven't bothered him or his children.  
He was present when the sky fell- is aware of the ancient technology

We get handed red wine even though we are all hesitant.  
"Hazle (daughter) come here"; she hisses something and casts a spell, resisting a counter spell. It's Silence\!

Time for combat\!  
Bramble gets to interrupt the daughter so Silence is gone.  
Ogra gets focused hard and Zinjaro gets grappled.

Lair action is up next
