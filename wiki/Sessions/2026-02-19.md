# Session: 2026-02-19

A fireball is completely ineffective, they appear to be immune to fire.  
Maxim's Cone of Cold is super effective.

The pixie does help us by casting Confusion on a kobold which causes it to run into [[wiki/Characters#Brambleberry|Brambleberry]].  
[[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] banishes the Efreeti.

We win the fight and Ogra offers Maxim some of the berries, but he doesn't eat them.

The fairy approaches Brambleberry and says we are all strange.  
She also refuses the berries.

Zinjaro ritually casts Detect Poison and sees Screeck has some poison vials in his armor. The berries are poisonous but we kinda knew that.

Bramble's flower turns back to normal (white and red).  
We continue to discuss the nature of the flower and how it detects unnatural things like technology.  
The pixie gives us a hint: They've been trying to deal with the kobolds in the mountain, they are growing but they can't go in to figure out what is going on. Nixie lost her ability to fly when she went inside.  
Six dryads went inside, but only 1 came out, green and then died.

Maxim asks about recognizing fey and the pixie says it's obvious. Maxim doesn't know that changelings are considered fey.  
The pixie goes to hide in Bramble's bag while the rest of us go back to sleep.

[[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] and Ogra stand watch, Tick comes out and chats about the repair work. It'll take a few days. She takes a canteen of something alcoholic, though it's specialized for the insect folk.  
Aolis tries it, but retches and throws up a little bit in his mouth. It's like 70-85 proof.  
Ogra does the same- it's even worse for him. Tick is perfectly fine drinking it. It's a difference in biology.  
She never would have given it to Nick. It was her 3rd mated partner. It was before the Schism.  
It's hard to find partners when she isn't in favor of killing others. 

Aolis doesn't know what the Schism is.   
Tick pulls out a tablet and shows Aolis' family. Apparently it was big news when Aolis left. Grandmother of Tick had mentioned it- trade relations were destroyed. Queen below thought the insects had smuggled Aolis.  
He asks "what is the Schism"?  
A difference in religious ideology, that the deity on top of the tower is not very nice and wants to destroy us. The higher ups are not a fan of this movement as that is what gave them power.  
Tick believes there is a god that has granted them technology, but the one in the tower is an evil one. She is in the minority.  
Most kin worship the Sky Father (the one on top of the tower).  
She describes how a platform comes down from the tower and delivers gifts to the hightecharchs. She's never seen what they are, though.  
The conversation ends and she injects a stimulant.
