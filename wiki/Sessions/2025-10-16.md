# Session: 2025-10-16

We find ourselves in a small room with some canvases.  
One depicts bodies in snow, another depicts a blurry person holding a brush standing in a blizzard.  
Another is a woman carrying a bundle under moonlight. Another is half empty, the rest depicts a building/wall crumbling. The last canvas is 3 individuals holding hands and walking away.  
Another painting is a nature portrait of the tundra.

Maxim rifles through some paintings. He finds a portrait of a young girl, wearing a crown of rose-like flowers. The ghost girl with us starts nodding. He also finds a latch under the shelf. He presses it and the section of shelf shifts slightly. It's a pathway\!

We go through and find a room with a piano, a desk, and some maps.  
The desk has a half drafted letter. It's in a language most of us don't recognize.  
"For Matilda... time was short... as you move west."  
Hard to tell how old it is.

There are some old maps, but we don't recognize where they are.  
[[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] recommends Ogra break the glass to get the maps. The air that comes out is stale.

Zinjaro reaches in to grab the maps. They are extremely delicate.  
The map denotes a single continent, but not one Zinjaro recognizes.  
Maxim remembers it- the Western-most continent, he doesn't remember the name, but it looks smaller.

We ask [[wiki/Characters#Thadius|Thadius]] to take a picture of the maps with his image-making device.

There are a few bars of music and Ogra tries, but can't make it.  
Maxim goes in to try and nails the performance check. We hear a rumbling somewhere.

[[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] checks the scrolls and just passes through the shelves.  
We split up: Aolis, Bramble, and Thadius go through the wall.  
Maxim, Zin, Ogra go look for the signs of the rumble.  
It appears we may have reached the same point- baddies\!

Our group is split up, but we are fighting them anyway.  
Banshee wails drop Bramble and Ogra down to zero.  
We kill the baddies, though some vampire spawns flee.  
Zinjaro heals Ogra and [[wiki/Characters#Brambleberry|Brambleberry]].

Maxim goes on his own and sees a room with some glowing lights.  
There are some coffins with chains, they seem solid like there is something in it. The rest of us join him as Maxim starts setting the coffins on fire.  
The chains shatter. More enemies appear\!
