# Session: 2026-06-25

The party continues the robot fight at the [[wiki/Quests#Current Objective: The Siege of Tesselia (April 2026)|Anti-Air gun]] church site, picking up directly from [[wiki/Sessions/2026-06-18|2026-06-18]].

## Combat
- A robot targets [[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] to grant everyone advantage on attacks against him, but [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] counters by dropping darkness via a **Hunger of Hadar** spell.
- Zinjaro casts **Bless** on Aolis, Bramble, Villhelm, and himself for extra accuracy.
- The party trades attacks with the remaining robots. [[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]], [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]], and even [[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] get badly wounded, forcing Zinjaro to spend time healing and the others to burn potions. **Mass Cure Wounds** helps significantly.
- Villhelm goes down right at the end of combat, just as Ogra kills the last robot. Zinjaro feeds him a handful of bananas (**Goodberries**) to revive him.

## Aftermath
The party takes a short rest to recover, but one area remains to explore. Maxim melts the door to the central area, and several of the party get shot at as it opens — starting another round of combat as the session ends.
