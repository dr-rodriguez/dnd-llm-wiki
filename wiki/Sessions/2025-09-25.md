# Session: 2025-09-25

We set up camp and [[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] prepares for the night by setting up Hallow via Divine Intervention.   
Fiends (demons and devils) will not be able to enter. Also as an extra effect, humanoids and fey can't be frightened. It's a big 60ft radius area that lasts until dispelled.  
We go to sleep in the big tent that Maxim bought.

In the second shift, [[wiki/Characters#Brambleberry|Brambleberry]] hears a whisper and goes outside.  
From the snowdrift, steps a beautiful looking woman. Bramble is immediately wary.  
She says: "Mother said you would be uneasy"  
[[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] also sees her. Bramble is wary  
Her name is Tundra. She is an aspect. She freezes the snow in mid-air.  
She has a gift from Brambleberry...  
She reaches and pulls out a bag, which she offers to Bramble.

Brambleberry changes\!  
Brambleberry now has only two legs- she is an Uma (Umamusume) now\!  
Her horseshoes turn to boots (immune to difficult terrain), and her clothing adapts to her new form. But she has no pants.  
"Mother may call on you in the future"  
Aolis originally held her up, but she let go. Bramble is able to stand on her own and take a few steps.  
Aolis goes to push her, but it's so ineffective it's like trying to push a boulder.  
They ask about Mother and Aolis realizes it's Mother Nature (god/goddess Natura, sort of pronounced "Natu-ra")

Bramble goes to Maxim to see if she can get some pants.  
The voice is Bramble's and the clothes are similar, but she looks completely different. He's startled, but Aolis assures him it's Bramble.  
Maxim searches through his bag for all his freshly pressed clothes to hand her a pair.  
It doesn't fit perfectly, but she uses some rope to tie it.  
She has horse ears now and a horse tail.  
Maxim asks if this is normal, but Bramble has never heard of it.  
Umas are the chosen guardians of Natu'ra. There are only ever 3 or less Umas on the Material Plane at any time. They have free will, but if Natu'ra calls they are expected to answer.

Maxim and Aolis take the last shift. They hear some thumping and use a spyglass to see what seems to be a bird hitting the barrier of Hallow.  
It keeps trying to enter, but Maxim leaves it alone.  
The bird is corrupted with demonic energy, which is why it can't enter the hallowed ground.

We all worry about the cart, but Bramble can actually still pull it. We agree to use actual horses in the city, but Bramble can still help out in the wilderness.  
We also argue about Maxim's commoner clothing, which he donates to Bramble.

We're delayed because Bramble can't get used to pulling the cart. She gives up and sits in the cart. We use Thaddius's horse to pull the cart.  
As we pass by the mountains, we see signs of dragon activity.  
Maxim can't determine how old, maybe a month old?  
Could they be corrupted dragons like the birds in the night?

We reach a forest of leafless trees. They look evil and corrupted.  
In the forest we find a lit campfire and some individuals.  
It's Team B\! (Maggie and the group)

Reminder: get some mushrooms and have Thaddius process them. Zinjaro might want to have that ready for any True Seeing.  
It appears the ruling may be that Changelings aren't magic so wouldn't be revealed by it- boo.
