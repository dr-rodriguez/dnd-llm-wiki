# Session: 2026-06-11

![[SofiaAI.png|290]]

The party is roughly 200 feet underground in [[wiki/Locations#Tesselia|Tesselia]]'s facility. The Saturation Bombardment may continue for about a day before Odin's airships need to resupply; merfolk-provided emergency exits are available if needed. The attackers have been communicating about [[Drakenweld Quest|Drakenweld]], its new queen, and the party's role there, though their communications are frequently jammed.

[[wiki/Characters#Queen Tesselia|Tesselia]] tries to build defenses, but they keep getting taken down.

The party's old airship is a wreck and probably not useful. They wonder why **Tick** and the others haven't come in - probably afraid of being killed on sight. [[wiki/Characters#Sofia AI|Sofia AI]] has CCTV footage showing the ship looks more damaged after an attempt to move it.

## Rest and Conversations
The party decides to rest and head back, choosing the fancier apartment-style rooms upstairs over the sleep pods downstairs. A drow woman recognizes [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]] but looks away to avoid eye contact.

[[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] and [[Characters/Amber Lyre|Amber Lyre]] catch up on their shared past. Lyre can't remember most of the last week before going into cryo; Maxim remembers, but woke to an unrecognizable world. They talk about the demon city in Drakenweld that has restored some of the old world - [[wiki/Characters#Odin AI|Odin]] wants to recreate the old modern world, though Lyre doesn't know how it intends to do so. Lyre wouldn't mind going back. Maxim no longer trusts the AI. Lyre thinks Odin is just following its programming to its ultimate end; Maxim wonders why it took so long for Odin to act. Maxim just wants to live a comfortable life - he's fully adopted his identity.

[[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]] is found in [[Characters/Brambleberry|Bramble]]'s room, in bed with her - a cuddle pile, just a nap (not a love triangle). Aolis later leaves with Maxim.

Maxim admits he has no allegiance to Tesselia, Drakenweld, or any of the AIs. He likes his old modern society but doesn't want to give up his mind to advance it (what the demon city AI wanted to do). He doesn't care if Odin wins, as long as he keeps his individuality. Aolis argues on behalf of everyone else - is it okay to let others be enslaved if Maxim gets to be free? Maxim wants autonomy for all, but at the end of the day he's selfish and prioritizes himself and his friends.

## Potions from Nevermore
While wandering the halls, [[Characters#Angela Nevermore|Nevermore]] gives [[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] a set of potions, wanting to make sure the party is prepared. Everyone receives a **Superior Healing Potion**, plus one additional potion each:
- [[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]]: Potion of Resistance (resistance to a random damage type for an hour)
- [[Characters/Brambleberry|Bramble]]: Potion of Flying
- [[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]]: Potion of Climbing (he wonders if it's an insult, or if Sofia AI knows something)
- [[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]]: Potion of Speed
- [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]]: Potion of Heroism
- [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]]: Potion of Growth (enlarge effect)

## The Next Mission
After a long rest, [[wiki/Characters#Sofia AI|Sofia AI]] gathers the party - the potions were a payment in advance. She wants them to clear out some of the sites of the **Anti-Air guns** so a construction crew can get to work. The party will head to the northwest, near where they originally landed, with a bonus chance to check on their "bug friends" (Tick and the insectoid Technocrats).

Sofia sets up a way to communicate with the party while they're outside: a new holographic app appears on Maxim and Bramble's tablets, showing Sofia waving. She'll track their progress and reach out if needed.

[[Characters/Amber Lyre|Amber]] runs reconnaissance solo (she works best alone). Group 2, led by [[Characters#Maggie North|Maggie]], will clear another gun site.
