# Session: 2025-12-18

Drowsy: similar to Slow (action or bonus action, no reactions). In 3 turns he will fall fully asleep.

Ogra sleep walks through the World Tree and comes out of the toilet.  
It's combat time against Mariquin.

Mariquin has an ability called Target Acquisition. She can copy the attack of someone else.

She copies first Bramble's psychic daggers, then Thaddius's gauntlets.

[[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] takes out Mariquin with a Fire Bolt.  
She has a small tablet, similar to what Maxim has. It's a cellphone.  
The computer starts wiping its memory but Maxim is able to use it to determine the Mayor is in the building.

Maxim takes Mariquin's form and we head out. We take an elevator to a break room and then another room. There are a bunch more demons and a new Mariquin shows up. She has short red hair and a techy shirt.  
It's another fight\! And against a lot more targets- a pit fiend, two incubus, two chain devils, a canoloth, a rakshasa, a night hag, and the second Mariquin.

[[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] banishes an incubus and the canoloth

Ogra has a Crown of Madness cast on him by Mariquin, compelling him to attack Zinjaro if within melee range.
