# Session: 2025-09-18

We continue the fight. Absolute Terror no longer stacks, which really helps out. [[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] is dragged to the water, which is bad, but [[wiki/Characters#Thadius|Thadius]] resists the Absolute Terror.

[[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] uses the Shard of Solar to summon daylight. It breaks Aolis's terror, shrinks the heads- breaking the grapple, and blinds them for one turn.

Alisha jumps into the water, a flash of light, she reaches Aolis, another flash of light, and sprouts wings\! She's an Aasimar. She brings Aolis to the boat, sheathes her sword, and holds up her hand and a halberd appears. She says she understands duality and is the Sky Dancer, like her father before her.

Maxim keeps casting Cone of Cold with Careful Spell to avoid hitting the party.  
Thadius climbs out of the water and fires a Lightning Bolt that kills the boss\!

Where the head was, there is now a black flame that seems to draw light in. It still burns.  
Thadius captures the flame in a glass jar. It gets warm and it will eventually melt the glass.

The avatar of Sol'Ar appears and everyone can see him.  
To prove himself, he makes Maxim incredibly drunk. He goes to the edge to throw up.

Ogra moves toward Alisha.  
Another person shows up- a woman bathed in moonlight. She speaks in two voices, alternate sides of her face light up (left- scolding, right- soft/motherly). The duality of the Moon- Luna.  
"We thought we saw a grown woman ready for revenge, but instead was a grieving child. We thought it was a mother we could wield." (It seems Luna was wrong about how ready Alisha was, and is apologizing)  
"Remember what we spoke of, time is short"  
Lore: people thought Luna was a child god, but it actually is the full god- just that it's a dual aspect.

The boat is split between the happy, partying side with Solar and the more somber side with Luna.

Solar (and Luna) vanishes, calling Zinjaro a Big Guy (despite him being only like 3 feet tall).

Alisha gives Ogra the Frost Iron Blade. She hugs him and Ogra lifts her up with his bear strength.

Alisha tells them she will leave us after we dock the ship.   
She thinks she may need to be Empress sooner than she expected.

Thadius sets up a clothesline to help dry people's clothes.

We take a short rest and arrive at the underground docks.   
There's some random folks there.  
We get asked if we are here on business and Maxim says so. He notices [[wiki/Characters#Brambleberry|Brambleberry]] and they sort of clock each other as roguish types.  
He asks us in Thieves' Cant and Brambleberry replies we're looking for a person.  
Some kind of interaction happened when he passed Brambleberry, but we don't know what.

We exit but it feels like the snow and the land hates us.  
She recommends we head to [[wiki/Locations#Anthrell|Anthrell]]- the western capital deeper into the demon lands.   
We may be able to use scrying.  
She needs to stop her mother from selling the kingdom to demons\!

Zinjaro prepares to Scry and looks west.  
The sun shines into a 5-6 story tall building. He sees a man, in a stone slab in tattered noble clothes and a thin sheet. He is bruised and bloodied. He seems almost broken.  
The room is barren, almost abandoned.  
There is a broken city scape outside the window.  
There is some deep red light behind the building- the snow clouds are a deeper red color.  
This appears to be a mid-sized building- there are other taller buildings. None of them that we can remark on for landmarks.   
He is on the edge of life, but just barely. He is not chained.

Maxim sends the mayor a message to get in touch.  
He doesn't know where he is- they keep moving him, he doesn't know why they want him.

Level up\! We are now level 11\.
