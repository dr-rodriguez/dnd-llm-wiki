# Session: 2026-05-28

## Overview
The party engaged in a climactic battle against the [[Lore#ScionInYellow|Scion in Yellow]]. The conflict escalated as the Scion warped reality, transporting the group to the Transcendent plane before finally being defeated by [[Characters#MaximusArkelius|Maxim]].

## Key Events
- **Battle with the Scion:** The [[Lore#ScionInYellow|Scion in Yellow]] attempted to dominate [[Characters#OgraOaksworn|Ogra]], but was resisted. [[Characters#Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] protected the party with *Aura of Purity*. The Scion displayed immunity to cold and poison.
- **Transcendence:** At half health, the Scion transported everyone to the Transcendent plane, where Wild Magic was in effect and terrain was difficult.
- **The Planets Crash:** The Scion manipulated celestial bodies to damage the party.
- **Wild Magic Chaos:** Wild Magic caused confusion in [[Characters#VillhelmEmberstoke|Villhelm]] and [[Characters#Brambleberry|Brambleberry]]. [[Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] gained resistance to all damage, and later [[Characters#MaximusArkelius|Maxim]] grew a third eye.
- **Slaad Summoning:** Two Blue Slaads were summoned into the fray but were eventually dispatched.
- **Victory:** [[Characters#MaximusArkelius|Maxim]] delivered the killing blow with a *Psychic Lance*.
- **The Aftermath:** The party was blinded and teleported back as Maxim opened the Scion's yellow book. The book's luster faded, and a chilling message remained: *"He has seen you. You have been noted."*
