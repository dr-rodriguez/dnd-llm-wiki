# Session: 2026-05-14

## Summary
The party continues their exploration of the tower, encountering strange creatures revealed by [[Lore#True Sight|True Sight]]. Combat ensues with various aberrations and outer-planar entities amidst flaring wild magic.

## Key Events
- **Strange Encounters:** With [[Lore#True Sight|True Sight]] active, the party sees creatures sharing their plane, including a [[Lore#Flumph|Flumph]], a [[Lore#Sticky Slime|Sticky Slime]], and a [[Lore#Goon Balloon|Goon Balloon]].
- **Sticky Situation:** [[Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] gets his hand stuck in a [[Lore#Sticky Slime|Sticky Slime]], which begins to dissolve a pencil pushed into it. [[Characters#Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] helps him free his hand.
- **The Tower Doors:** The party finds three doors: one humming with magic, one with scratching sounds, and a silent one with a different rune.
- **Outer Plane Anchors:** They discover conjuration circles acting as dual anchor points between the Material and Outer Planes.
- **Wild Magic Surge:** [[Characters#Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]]'s illusion magic triggers a wild magic effect (reincarnation if he dies). [[Characters#Aolis|Aolis]]'s spell makes him 3 years younger. [[Characters#Zinjaro|Zinjaro]]'s fireball causes him to leave a trail of moss and flowers.
- **Astral Voyage:** [[Characters#Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] briefly vanishes to the [[Lore#Astral Plane|Astral Plane]], seeing floating fish in a star ocean.
- **Combat:**
    - Defeated [[Lore#Grell|Grell]].
    - Encountered a [[Lore#Neh-thalggu|Neh-thalggu]] (Brain Collector).
    - Faced an [[Lore#Eye Monger|Eye Monger]], a swarm of [[Lore#Cranium Rats|Cranium Rats]], and a [[Lore#Death Kiss|Death Kiss]].

## Creatures
- **Flumph:** Docile creatures that protect jewels. One followed [[Characters#Brambleberry|Bramble]] and later wrapped itself around [[Characters#Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]].
- **Sticky Slime:** Sewer-dwelling creatures with mucus coating.
- **Goon Balloon:** Cactus-eyed, chaotic evil creatures.
- **Grell:** Brain-like creatures with bird beaks and tentacles.
- **Neh-thalggu:** Brain-collecting aberrations.
- **Eye Monger:** One-eyed stone aberrations.
- **Cranium Rats:** Telepathic rat swarms.
- **Death Kiss:** A beholder-kin that drains blood.
