# Session: 2026-06-18

The party heads out to the [[wiki/Quests#Current Objective: The Siege of Tesselia (April 2026)|Anti-Air guns]] to clear them of hostiles, continuing [[wiki/Characters#Sofia AI|Sofia AI]]'s mission. They attempt to be stealthy but fail, getting delayed and arriving about an hour later than intended.

## The Church Site
They arrive at what appears to have been a church. There is a fountain with a **chimera statue** and several **hippocampus statues**.

[[Characters/Brambleberry|Bramble]] unlocks a door, but on opening it gets shot at by enemy **robot troops**. She closes the door quickly, but takes a hit.

## Combat
- [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] places a Minor Illusion of Brambleberry at the door to confuse the robots, while [[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] preps a Sacred Flame in case he spots an enemy.
- A robot peeks through the door, avoids the Sacred Flame, and doesn't realize the illusory Bramble is fake. The robots throw a **flashbang** — Maxim and [[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]] are blinded and stunned. Another robot shoots the illusion and realizes it's fake.
- [[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] rages as another robot comes through a side door. Zinjaro, shot at, uses **Light Snare** to restrain the attacker, casts another Sacred Flame, and moves away. [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]] casts Witch Bolt and summons their **Hound of Ill Omen**. Bramble throws her daggers into the room.
- The robots remotely call for backup. Villhelm recovers from the flashbang and moves to punch the side robot. With several robots streaming from a corner, Zinjaro throws a **Fireball** at the corner hoping to catch some. Bramble takes out one robot with her daggers.
- A **large robot** breaks through a wall and fires at Villhelm.

The fight is still ongoing as the session ends.
