# Session: 2026-04-23

We continue the fight.

[[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] uses his dexterous feet to contort himself and reach his holy symbol to cast Freedom of Movement (VSM). His hands were tied behind him with laser manacles, but now he is free.

[[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]] recovers from the blindness and starts punching the robots.

[[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] and [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]] have started fighting inside. A bout of flame destroys a door (Aolis cast a spell).

Zinjaro successfully Banishes 3 of the robots to control the battlefield for a bit (they’ll return to this plane after a minute).

Maxim summons Evard’s Black Tentacles.

Friendly reinforcements arrive!
[[Characters/Amber Lyre|Amber Lyre]]- a bard, they belt out a Healing Word to help Zinjaro.
They take out a pistol and join the battle.

Amber hands over some cylindrical devices to [[Characters/Brambleberry|Bramble]] and asks where the banished opponents were. It’s like a bear trap without teeth, but with electrical wiring.

When concentration ends, the robots return but are knocked unconscious. 
She immediately hugs and cries to Maxim.
She thought no one survived the pods. She de-shifts-- she’s a changeling, just like Maxim!

She leads us inside- these are the people we were meant to find.
They had a sending stone but hadn’t used it. It doesn’t seem to work, but Maxim is able to send a message.

[[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] takes a giant gatling gun, though it’s cumbersome. Maxim and Villhelm take some of the smaller pistols.
Villhelm asks Amber for help repairing the pistol.
After a few hours she repairs the pistols. Ogra offers the gatling gun for parts.

Ogra pulls out the Fabricator and Amber lights up. She hasn’t seen a working one in a long time. We convert the gatling gun to 75 scrap.
We have 475 scrap.

Bramble gets Soul Leather armor from the PortaFab. It’s a +1 leather armor that grants Invisibility as an action.
Villhelm gets Kinetic Accelerator, which gives additional force damage if they move 10 feet (1 stack/10 feet).
We clear out our scrap.

We eat a bit of soup.
