# Session: 2026-05-07

[[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] realizes [[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] is free of the madness and releases him. They join the others inside.
Zinjaro explains Ogra was the one to cure him. He tries to grapple [[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]], but he wiggles free.

We head to the back trying to find the man in yellow who has disappeared in the back.
[[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] mentally persuades [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]] to break free and it works - they are free and drop invisibility. He asks Maxim for a hug.

We go in the back and see the yellow man, we follow upstairs.

It's a sitting room.
There is a door with no handle, only a seam with a symbol.
Ogra attempts to break it, but is launched back.
[[Characters/Brambleberry|Bramble]] investigates the symbol. It's glowing, no obvious way to open from this side. She notices a section of the wall is different - it's a secret door.

Aolis knows the rune/symbol on the door is something of the **Outer Planes**.
Zinjaro knows it's connected to something, but doesn't know to which.
Just that it is related to something about Knowledge and that studying the Outer Planes is illegal.

Bramble holds the book to the door. The door reacts, a rune glows on the book. The door doesn't open.
Aolis copies down the rune on a piece of paper.

Through the stone doorway is a bedroom.
There is a desk with some papers. They refer to a person in yellow trying to sell something - a leatherbound book that was blank.
The yellow individual left the book behind.
Next pages: the owner flips through the book again and there is now writing - short stories that are not connected. 
The later page is ramblings of a madman. Every 4th scrawling is in yellow ink. Every 6th word is "yellow". Every 8th word is "book".
At the end it's just "yellow" and "book" over and over.

Aolis casts **Hold Person** and paralyzes Villhelm.
Maxim investigates the book - he feels it's related to the symbol of the door but can't make more.
They open the book - there is a bright flash. There is a Fear effect but only Villhelm fails. Some nasty enemies with lots of eyes and mouths also appear... **Gibbering Mouther**.
We all get the effect of **True Sight**.

Around the monsters the ground is like putty and if we're nearby we have to make strength saves or get stuck (Zinjaro gets stuck).
Zinjaro attempts to **Banish** the two Gibbering Mouthers... but it doesn't work. Something is anchoring them to this plane (we think it's the book).

Aolis realizes they are immune to being knocked down due to their amorphous forms.
One of them starts gibbering which will cause bad effects.
