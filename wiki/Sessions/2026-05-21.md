# Session: 2026-05-21

Combat continues in the tower. [[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] takes significant damage from swarms of [[Lore#Cranium Rats|cranium rats]]. [[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]] disengages from a [[Lore#Death Kiss|Death Kiss]] to enter the room, with [[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] closing the door behind them. An [[Lore#Eye Monger|Eye Monger]] unsuccessfully attempts to attack [[Characters/Brambleberry|Brambleberry]]. Villhelm's fire breath accidentally ignites some coffins in the room.

Outside, [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]], his hound, and [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] fire at the [[Lore#Death Kiss|Death Kiss]] while the rest of the party deals with the [[Lore#Cranium Rats|cranium rats]] and the [[Lore#Eye Monger|Eye Monger]] inside. [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] casts *Wall of Fire*, which begins to spread through the carpet.

[[Characters/Brambleberry|Brambleberry]] successfully kills the [[Lore#Eye Monger|Eye Monger]] with her psychic daggers. [[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]] extinguishes the fire on the coffins while [[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] provides healing (*Aura of Vitality*, *Divine Spark*, *Healing Word*). After combat, [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] uses *Control Flames* to put out the carpet fire. [[Characters/Brambleberry|Brambleberry]] opens a locked coffin, but it is empty.

The party discovers a **green stone** that is hot to the touch but does not emit radiation. It is believed to be from the [[Lore#Outer Planes|Outer Planes]], possibly a "warming stone" that has absorbed strange energy.

In the hallway, [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] hears a whisper ("the book...") and feels a compulsion to open his yellow book. It contains the phrase "**Ad Astra**" (To the stars). When he attempts to psychically communicate this, he notices a new room with runes on the floor. [[Characters/Aolis|Aolis]] casts *Comprehend Languages* to help Maxim write down the words, but as Maxim writes them, they change to "**Mors Imperii**" (Death of the empire). The book eventually displays the word "**UP**", and the party is teleported by the glowing runes in the floor.

They arrive in a room with a **[[Lore#Yellow Cloak|figure in a yellow cloak]]**. The figure speaks with three discordant voices (two male, one female). To Aolis, the voices sound like his mother and sisters screaming; to Ogra, like a crackling fire; and to Maxim, they are generic. The figure delivers a prophecy:

> "**In morte imperii deus noster de caelo descendet.**"
> (In the death of the empire, our god will descend from space.)

The figure begins to float. [[Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] and [[Characters/Villhelm Emberstoke|Villhelm]] observe "Outer Plane food" on a table, including fluorescent blue plants and purple substances. The yellow-cloaked figure then transforms into a creature with numerous tentacles.
