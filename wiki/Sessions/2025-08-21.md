# Session: 2025-08-21

We buy some supplies  
[[wiki/Characters/Zinjaro|Zinjaro]] buys 300g diamond dust  
Maxim buys a big tent  
[[wiki/Characters#Aolis|Aolis]] buys treated wood

We decided to go by the road down to the fork.  
We set up camp, Maxim makes the fire purple. Maxim gets naked to shock us.  
[[wiki/Characters#Thadius|Thadius]] and Ogra gets first watch, Aolis gets second

During Aolis' shift a man and woman with wyvern mounts approaches the tent. They claim they are running away, a "field trip"  
It's Alisha and the Professor\!

Aolis invites them inside. Chat a bit about learning from experience.  
Aolis starts to panic- He Knows. He asks how long it has been since they left. He knows their name- Arete.  
He says he received a sending from her Mother, but he told her he hadn't seen Aolis.

Aolis wakes up Maxim, he makes a bit of a scene before getting dressed.  
Maxim asks about the dracoshillings. The Professor says it's very old, likely from the founding of the city. Would need to find a collector, but likely not in [[wiki/Locations#Drakenweld|Drakenweld]]. Maybe in [[wiki/Locations#Moren|Moren]]?

The Professor says in an old drow dialect: "Her eyes are always watching. Always make them blind". The Professor rides away.

Alisha asks if they fuck, comments on the lack of private rooms for the hot tent.  
Alisha prepares breakfast and announces she will be joining us.

Alisha tells us about a shortcut through the tundra.   
Subterranean waterways maintained by the roguish types.  
Alisha leads us back north and into the tundra to reach these waterways.

Maxim asks Alisha about her plans to govern. She doesn't care- more worried about the current war.

There may be a leader to the demons now, but haven't been able to confirm.

While we walk through the snow we see a big creature. A large snake woman with 6 arms and a blade in each- a Marilith. She challenges Alisha and we start prepping for battle.

Zinjaro summons a Celestial Defender- an ape-like angel with wings.   
Next session it will take a bonus action to attack.
