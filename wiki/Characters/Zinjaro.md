## Appearance
Just under 3' tall with white fur and skin flaps for gliding.

## Backstory
Zinjaro served as a guide for a mountain community but was always fascinated by the grand tales of adventurers. He views his innate talent for navigation and weather prediction as divine blessings from his deity, a god of the sun and travel. 

He practices a unique, hedonistic interpretation of his faith, believing that enjoying life's "vices"—fine wine, rich food, and festivals—is a form of worship, as "the sun shines on all." A local prophecy regarding a "guide touched by the sun" has further fueled his belief in a grand destiny, which he hopes involves a beach-side villa as a base of operations.

### Key Events
- **Siege of Tessalia:** In April 2026, Zinjaro was identified as a "known dissident" by automaton occupation forces. After unsuccessfully attempting to use **Maggie North** as a false identity, he was engaged in combat, grappled, and arrested.

## Personality
- **Optimistic:** Believes the sun's light will always guide him.
- **Hedonistic:** Enjoys life's pleasures, justifying them as divine worship.
- **Self-Justifying:** Adept at interpreting faith and prophecies to suit his own desires.
- **Slightly Misguided:** Likely exaggerates his role in the "sun-touched guide" prophecy.
- **Wanderlustful:** Deeply desires to explore and experience the world.
