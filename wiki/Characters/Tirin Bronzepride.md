## Backstory
Tirin Bronzepride is a Leonin barbarian who joined the party following **Dumo**'s retirement. Known for his impatience and fierce fighting style, he famously dueled **Soren** to prove his martial superiority. 

In December 2024, Tirin was gifted a powerful axe called **"The Broken Promise."** Tragically, he fell in battle during the final confrontation with the **Athena AI** in January 2025.
