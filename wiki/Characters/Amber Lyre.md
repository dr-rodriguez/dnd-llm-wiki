## Backstory / Events
Amber Lyre is a changeling bard who was discovered by the party in April 2026 during a fight against automatons. She survived the "pods" just like [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maximus Arkelius]] and was relieved to find another survivor. She aided the party in combat and helped repair their scavenged technology. She is familiar with ancient tech, recognizing the Fabricator/PortaFab when [[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] presented it.
- **April 2026:** Amber revealed she crash-landed from the orbital station. She successfully repaired a communicator and contacted the Odin AI, receiving instructions from him.
- **June 11, 2026:** Caught up with [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maxim]] about their shared past, though she can't remember most of the last week before going into cryo. She thinks [[wiki/Characters#Odin AI|Odin]] is just following its programming to its ultimate end and wouldn't mind going back to the old modern world. She volunteered to run reconnaissance solo for the party's next mission (clearing Anti-Air gun sites), as she works best alone.
