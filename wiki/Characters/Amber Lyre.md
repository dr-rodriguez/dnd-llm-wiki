## Backstory / Events
Amber Lyre is a changeling bard who was discovered by the party in April 2026 during a fight against automatons. She survived the "pods" just like [[Characters/Maximus Arkelius|Maximus Arkelius]] and was relieved to find another survivor. She aided the party in combat and helped repair their scavenged technology. She is familiar with ancient tech, recognizing the Fabricator/PortaFab when [[Characters/Ogra Oaksworn|Ogra]] presented it.
