## Backstory
Nova is a Warforged paladin affectionately nicknamed **"Rez-bot"** for his ability to repeatedly revive his companions. He discovered a deep personal connection to the **Athena AI**, who referred to him as her "brother." Athena offered Nova "freedom" from his current existence, but he chose to remain with his party. 

Nova was a pillar of the group, often organizing morale-boosting events, such as a holiday festival and a wrestling match against a brown bear. He was killed during the final battle with the **Athena AI** when he was forcibly infected with a nanovirus and frozen in virtual space.
