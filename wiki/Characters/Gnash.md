## Backstory
Gnash was an orphan Lizardfolk barbarian who joined the party after **Idecca**'s disappearance. He was known for his physical prowess, most notably grappling a shark during an underwater mission. 

Tragically, Gnash was killed by **Ashton Whitemore (the Regent)**. His halberd now rests on a mantle in the **Adventurer’s Guild** as a memorial to his service. **Soren** later named a familiar "Gnashy" in his honor.
