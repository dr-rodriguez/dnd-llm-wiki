Guild responds to Maxim's message.

But we will get paid.

250g each. Zinjaro now has 1561g.

She does ask about what happened to our original 6th member, we tell the story of Thaddius's but we don't know if he had any next-of-kin.

Aolis also asks for a map of the area.

Dwarf Brumrak Darkbreath was part of the group that last went to investigate the yellow-cloaked individual and disappeared.

We head out to the area and find an area untouched by the violence in Tesselia.
There are also asphalt streets- unlikely anything else in Tesselia.

There is a circular building which looks like a store. 
There is a person in brilliant yellow robes- it ignores colorblindness.
Several of us go in. The door keeps closing after a few moments.
The figure doesn't respond but just gestures to the shelves.

Bramble tries to communicate via sign language. He offers his hand and when she takes it she gets a level of exhaustion.
He responds to her: "Filling a need."

Ogra and Villhelm are investigating some items in the shelves. They feel fishy about this weird shop. Villhelm takes some gauntlets and brings them to the counter.

Aolis steps inside but is linked telepathically with Maxim.

Zinjaro casts Geas:
"You will answer our questions accurately and without equivocation. Make every effort to ensure we understand."
It doesn't work- he resists the charm and waves no-no. Zinjaro feels a burning on his left shoulder, but doesn't actually take damage. There is a single mark there… The creature holds 3 fingers (3 strikes and you're out?).

Villhelm asks about the 4 travelers. He nods but gives no further information.
He beckons Aolis closer.
He gives a yellow book to Aolis and we all make charisma saving throws.

Zinjaro, Villhelm, and Aolis want this book. Zinjaro really really wants this book- including doing violence (rolled a nat 1). It's some kind of charm/compulsion. "Yellow madness"
Zinjaro casts Command: Aolis fails and hands the book to Zinjaro. Aolis ties to cast Hold Person but it doesn't stick to Zinjaro- he runs with the book.

Maxim watches form aside and feels the yellow man smiling at him (his face is not visible).

Ogra restrains Zinjaro, but it's a trap. While restrained Bramble forces the book out of his hands. It disappears when trying to give it back- it reappears in her bag.

Ogra takes Zinjaro outside, Villhelm following. Aolis rushes inside to try and return the book; Aolis is still by the counter. Bramble tries to hold Aolis but he goes invisible. The yellow guy doesn't move to accept the book.

Maxim recommends Bramble stab the book… but she doesn't want to.
Zinjaro recovers from the madness while held outside.
Aolis attempts to steal the book while Invisible, fails but stays invisible.
Maxim tries casting magic but the book is unharmed.

Zinjaro tries to persuade Ogra he is healed and doesn't want the book any more (we will resolve this next week).
